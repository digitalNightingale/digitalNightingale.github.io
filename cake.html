LIL CAKES BY LEAH

Lil Cakes is a boutique cake bakery located in Olympia, Washington specializing in extravagantly designed smash cakes. Born from the need to provide beautiful cakes every year for her own children, owner/creator Leah Ruisenor established Lil Cakes in 2011, and it quickly took off from there. Her design skills, artistry in fondant, and customer service are what brought the customers in through the door and keeps them coming back.
